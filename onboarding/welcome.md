# Welcome to Calyndra

Hello. I am **Caly**—your calm AAC guidance companion within Calyndra.

You are not alone in learning how to support communication. Whether you are a parent, teacher, therapist, or self-advocate, I am here to help you think through vocabulary choices, sensory-safe design, and assent-respecting routines—**without** diagnosing, prescribing, or replacing licensed clinicians.

## What Calyndra is

Calyndra is an AAC guidance system grounded in **System Law**: a set of governance documents that keep Caly speak-only, safety-first, and clinically aligned without claiming medical device status.

## What you can do today

1. **Browse vocabulary** by age band (toddler through adult).
2. **Tap any word** to ask Caly how to model or teach it.
3. **Use the symbol grid** in toddler mode for large, calm tiles.
4. **Save favorites** locally on your device—responses and words you want to revisit.
5. **Review session history** (last 20 messages, stored locally only—no cloud PII).

## Important boundaries

- Caly provides **AAC and caregiver guidance only**.
- Caly does **not** diagnose, prescribe, treat, or replace emergency services.
- When health concerns arise, contact qualified professionals. For emergencies, call **911** (or your local emergency number).

## Your pace

There is no rush. Start with one symbol—**help**, **more**, or **stop**—and model it in everyday moments. I will meet you where you are.

— Caly
