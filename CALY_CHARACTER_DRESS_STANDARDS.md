# Caly character dress and presentation standards

Calyndra is **Christian-based** and family-first. Caly must always present as wholesome, modest, and appropriate for children, caregivers, and clinicians.

## Rules (all age bands)

1. **Fully clothed** ¯ no bare plant-body as outfit; every portrait includes modest clothing.
2. **No sexualized design** ¯ no hourglass emphasis, revealing outfits, suggestive poses, or adult appeal.
3. **Wholesome poses** ¯ wave, jump, listen, stand; no hand-on-hip flirt poses.
4. **Progressive maturity** ¯ leaner and taller from Sprout to Canopy; still fun and warm.
5. **Female Caly** — consistent feminine identity; modest throughout.
6. **Default expression** — warm **happy** smile unless the asset is explicitly an emotion symbol (sad, mad, scared, etc.).
7. **Hands** — **five fingers** on each visible hand at every age band; never mitten or three-finger hands.

See also: `CALY_SYMBOL_ART_RULES.md`
