# Calyndra symbol art direction (original IP)

Inspired-by aesthetics only — **no copies** of Lucas & Friends, Disney, Nickelodeon, or other trademarked characters.

| Style | Band | Visual language |
|-------|------|-----------------|
| **sprout** | Toddler 2–4 | Chunky rounded shapes, pastel mint/sky palette, thick 8px outlines, friendly blob mascot accents, big eyes on objects, soft gradients |
| **quest** | Child 5–12 | Warm golden storybook frame, sparkle stars, adventure banner corners, saturated but cozy colors, magical forest greens |
| **spark** | Teen 13–17 | Bold slime-lime (#7CFC00) accents on dark charcoal, dynamic diagonal streaks, graffiti-energy shapes, neon edge glow |
| **core** | Adult 18+ | Flat HUD/game UI icons, minimal palette, crisp 2px lines, professional dark slate background |

Each symbol PNG: 512×512, centered pictogram + subtle style frame, transparent-friendly edges.

Regenerate: `python scripts/generate_word_pictures_v2.py`
