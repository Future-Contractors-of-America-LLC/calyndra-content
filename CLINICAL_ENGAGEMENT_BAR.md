# Clinical engagement bar

**Applies to:** every kid-facing and caregiver-facing surface in Calyndra (web app, demo, marketing, chat, games, routines, Support tab).

## Assent-first

- **Skip always** Ś every game round, routine step, song, video, and chat turn can be skipped without penalty.
- **Celebrate attempts** Ś partial taps, glances, and tries count; errorless learning option where applicable.
- **No coercion** Ś never pressure, shame, or withhold preferred activities to force compliance.
- **Pause anywhere** Ś sensory and routine flows respect stop/pause.

## Disclaimers (required)

Surfaces must include or link to:

> Educational support tool. Not medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, or emergency services. Does not replace licensed clinicians (SLP, OT, BCBA, developmental pediatrician).

- Not FDA-cleared, not a medical device.
- Session summaries are **transparency artifacts**, not clinical records or treatment outcomes.

## Language aligned with practice

Use terminology familiar to SLP / OT / BCBA / developmental pediatrics:

| Concept | Calyndra phrasing |
|---------|-------------------|
| Modeling words | **Aided language stimulation** Ś caregiver models on symbols while speaking |
| Visual schedules | **Visual supports** / TEACCH-style routines |
| Gentle success | **Errorless learning option** Ś celebrate attempts, no penalty for wrong taps |
| Caregiver cues | **Caregiver modeling cues** Ś plain prompts for how to model at home |
| Child choice | **Assent-first** Ś skip, pause, say no |
| Floortime | **Follow my lead** Ś join topic, match pace (not licensed DIR therapy) |
| Progress data | **Session summary for care-team sharing** Ś on-device, not EMR |

Avoid: "therapy app," "treatment plan," "clinical outcomes," "diagnose," "prescribe," "FDA-approved."

## Copy templates

**Kid celebrate (attempt):** "You tried! That counts."

**Kid skip:** "All done Ś nice choice."

**Caregiver boundary:** "This helps you practice at home. Your clinician leads treatment decisions."

**Emergency redirect:** "If someone is in danger, call emergency services or your care team Ś Calyndra cannot help in emergencies."
