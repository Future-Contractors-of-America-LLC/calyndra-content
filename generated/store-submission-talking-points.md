**Calyndra AAC App – Store Submission Talking Points**

---

**What is Calyndra AAC?**
- Calyndra is an Augmentative and Alternative Communication (AAC) app designed to support individuals with complex communication needs.
- Provides a customizable, symbol-based interface for expressing wants, needs, and ideas.
- Built with evidence-informed, user-centered design principles for accessibility and ease of use.
- Supports communication across a range of abilities, including non-speaking users and those with speech or language challenges.

**What Calyndra AAC is NOT:**
- Calyndra is NOT a medical device and does not diagnose, treat, or replace clinical care.
- The app does NOT provide emergency services or clinical decision-making.
- Calyndra does NOT claim FDA clearance or regulatory certification as a medical device.

**Age-Adaptive User Interface:**
- Features dynamic UI that adapts to the user's age and developmental stage.
- Toddler, child, teen, and adult modes adjust vocabulary, visual complexity, and interaction style for safety and comfort.
- Sensory-friendly options minimize overstimulation and support diverse sensory needs.

**Privacy and Data Security:**
- Calyndra is committed to privacy: no user data is sold or shared for advertising.
- All communication content remains on the device unless the user or caregiver chooses to export or share.
- Compliant with major privacy standards (e.g., COPPA, GDPR) to protect children and vulnerable users.
- No account creation required for core functionality; optional features are transparent and consent-based.

**Target Users:**
- Designed for non-speaking individuals, those with speech or language disabilities, and anyone who benefits from AAC support.
- Suitable for use by children, teens, and adults, as well as caregivers, educators, and therapists supporting communication.
- Flexible for home, school, and community environments.

**Key Features:**
- Customizable vocabulary sets and symbol libraries.
- Simple, intuitive navigation for all ages.
- Optional caregiver controls and transparency tools.
- Evidence-informed reinforcement and assent-aware interaction patterns.
- Offline functionality for privacy and reliability.

**Safety and Support:**
- Calyndra encourages caregiver involvement and transparency.
- For medical or emergency needs, consult qualified professionals—Calyndra does not replace clinical guidance.
