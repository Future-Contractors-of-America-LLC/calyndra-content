# The seven support modalities in Calyndra

Calyndra is built as a **comprehensive autism support platform** — not a single-trick AAC app. Each modality is educational carryover aligned with common clinical practice. None replace licensed therapy, diagnosis, or emergency services.

## 1. AAC and symbol communication
Picture words, aided language stimulation, and Caly voice modeling. Tap a symbol, celebrate attempts, skip anytime.

## 2. TEACCH routines
Visual schedules, first/then boards, and printable morning or bedtime flows with Caly voice prompts.

## 3. OT sensory profile
Calm colors, reduced motion, larger tiles, quieter voice, high contrast, and quiet celebrations — self-directed, always optional.

## 4. ABA-aligned progress
On-device session summaries (games, routines, chat activity) for honest sharing with your BCBA. **Not a clinical record.**

## 5. DIR floortime play
Join their topic, match pace, celebrate the try, follow their lead — assent-first play prompts.

## 6. CBT coping (teen and adult)
Breathing ladder, grounding, and one-line thought reframing. Educational self-help only.

## 7. PT movement breaks
Gentle jump, stretch, and wiggle cards with a skippable timer. Not a workout prescription.

---

**Clinical bar:** Assent-first everywhere. Skip always. Plain language for caregivers. When in doubt, defer to your licensed team.
