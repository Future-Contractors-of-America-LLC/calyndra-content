# Module 1: Your First Symbol — Help

**Time:** 5 minutes daily for 5 days  
**Audience:** Caregivers with toddlers or early communicators  
**Goal:** Model **help** in natural routines without demanding imitation

## Day 1 — Notice
Watch when the person needs assistance (opening a container, reaching a toy). Do not introduce the symbol yet. Just notice 3 moments.

## Day 2 — Model once
At one moment of need, tap **help** on the board and say calmly: "Help." Assist them. Stop. No quiz.

## Day 3 — Model three times
Model **help** at breakfast, play, and cleanup. Wait 5 seconds after each model.

## Day 4 — Offer the board
Place the board within reach during one routine. If they ignore it, that is okay.

## Day 5 — Honor any tap
If they tap **help** (or any symbol), respond immediately when safe. Say: "You asked for help."

## Reflection questions for caregivers
- Did I wait long enough after modeling?
- Did I honor **stop** or **no** if it appeared?
- Was the environment calm (low noise, stable lighting)?

## Caly check-in prompt
Ask Caly: *"How do I model help without creating pressure?"*
