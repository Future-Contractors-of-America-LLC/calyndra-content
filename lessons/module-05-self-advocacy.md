# Module 5: Teen & Adult Self-Advocacy

**Time:** Self-paced  
**Audience:** Teens, adults, and supporters  
**Goal:** Use AAC for autonomy, accommodations, and health communication boundaries

## Core self-advocacy symbols
- **my choice**
- **slow down**
- **privacy please**
- **I don't consent**
- **accommodation**
- **I need a break**

## Scenario practice

### Medical appointment (communication only)
Model: "I have pain here." [ **pain** ]  
Model: "I need you to explain more." [ **explain** ]  
Reminder: Caly supports communication planning—not diagnosis or treatment advice.

### Workplace or college
Model: **accommodation** + **write it down** for meeting notes.  
Supporters: accept written AAC as valid participation.

### Social settings
**not now** and **no** are complete sentences. No justification required.
