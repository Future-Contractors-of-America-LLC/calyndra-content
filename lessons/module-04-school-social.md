# Module 4: School & Social Words

**Time:** 15 minutes, 3 days per week  
**Audience:** Caregivers of school-age children (5–12)  
**Goal:** Support participation with **my turn**, **break**, **please**, and question words

## School readiness (not academic drilling)
Model symbols that reduce friction:
- **break** before overload
- **my turn** during games
- **bathroom** with predictable permission
- **what** / **where** to ask for clarification

## Role-play (low pressure)
Use dolls or stuffed animals. One asks **my turn**, the other waits. No scores.

## Partnering with school staff
Share this one-page note:
> "These symbols are honored as communication. Please allow break and bathroom requests without behavioral penalties when safe."
