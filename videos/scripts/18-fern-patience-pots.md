# Fern's Patience Pots (toddler, ~10 min, 100 scenes)

**Series:** Caly and Friends  
**Band:** Toddler (Caly Sprout, 2-4)  
**Friend:** Fern the fox  
**Mentor:** Elder Oak  
**Christian lesson:** Good things grow when we wait with hope. (Galatians 6:9 - do not grow weary in doing good).

## Story arc

| Act | Scenes | Beats |
|-----|--------|-------|
| Potting day | 1-15 | Empty pots, seeds, Caly Sprout labels |
| Plant and water | 16-35 | Dig, drop seed, water can song |
| Waiting games | 36-55 | Day counter, peek-not-pick rule, Elder Oak story |
| First sprout | 56-75 | Tiny green, cheer, measure with ruler |
| Patience party | 76-100 | All pots checked, celebrate waiting well |

## Key scenes

| # | Visual | Dialogue |
|---|--------|----------|
| 1 | Garden table with clay pots and seed packets | Today we plant seeds and practice patience. |
| 2 | Fern drops a seed, Caly covers with soil | Plant, water, and wait. Good things take time. |
| 3 | Elder Oak tells a story under the branches | Elder Oak says hope grows in quiet waiting. |
| 4 | Calendar pages flip, Fern checks pots daily | Every day we look, but we don't pull yet. |
| 5 | Tiny green sprout breaks through soil | Look! It grew! Waiting was worth it! |
| 6 | Fern and Caly do a patience dance | You waited so well. I'm proud of you. |
| 7 | Garden sunset, pots in a row | See you tomorrow, little sprouts! |
