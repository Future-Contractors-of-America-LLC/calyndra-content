# Reed's Mentor Circle (tween, ~20 min, 200 scenes)

**Series:** Caly and Friends  
**Band:** Tween (Caly Sprig, 9-12)  
**Friend:** Reed the owl  
**Mentor:** Elder Oak  
**Christian lesson:** Learning from elders and teaching younger ones completes the circle. (Titus 2:3-4 - older teaching what is good).

## Story arc

| Act | Scenes | Beats |
|-----|--------|-------|
| Circle forms | 1-30 | Reed invites multi-age circle under Elder Oak |
| Elder lesson | 31-70 | Elder Oak shares tree ring wisdom |
| Reed teaches | 71-110 | Reed teaches Pip listening game |
| Pip teaches | 111-150 | Pip teaches joy clap, everyone learns |
| Circle close | 151-200 | Each friend states one lesson learned |
