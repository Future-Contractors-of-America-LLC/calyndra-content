# Fern's Garden Share (toddler, ~10 min, 100 scenes)

**Series:** Caly and Friends  
**Band:** Toddler (Caly Sprout, 2–4)  
**Friend:** Fern the fox  
**Mentor:** Elder Oak  
**Christian lesson:** Sharing joy makes friendship grow (Acts 20:35 — give and it feels good).

## Story arc

| Act | Scenes | Beats |
|-----|--------|-------|
| Garden tour | 1–15 | Meet Fern, seeds, watering can |
| First share | 16–35 | Fern shares berries; Caly models MORE and THANK YOU |
| Feelings | 36–55 | Happy/sad when share refused; Elder Oak mediates |
| Practice | 56–80 | Turn-taking game with picture words |
| Celebration | 81–100 | Garden parade, song, blessing |

## Key scenes

| # | Visual | Dialogue |
|---|--------|----------|
| 1 | Sprout garden gate opens | "Welcome to Fern's garden!" |
| 2 | Fern with watering can | "I grow flowers for my friends." |
| 3 | Caly holds SHARE symbol | "Sharing means both of us can smile." |
| 4 | Elder Oak bench | "Kind words are like sunshine for the heart." |
| 5 | Berry basket, two friends | "One for you, one for me — thank you!" |
| 6 | Word parade: share, more, please | "Tap SHARE when you want to give." |
| 7 | Celebration confetti | "Every kind share is a win!" |

## Interactive tap-along

- Chapters at ~120s, ~300s, ~480s: tap SHARE, tap THANK YOU, garden parade.
