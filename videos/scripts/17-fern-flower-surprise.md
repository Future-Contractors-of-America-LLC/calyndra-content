# Fern's Flower Surprise (toddler, ~10 min, 100 scenes)

**Series:** Caly and Friends  
**Band:** Toddler (Caly Sprout, 2-4)  
**Friend:** Fern the fox  
**Mentor:** Elder Oak  
**Christian lesson:** Small surprises can brighten someone's whole day. (Proverbs 11:25 - generous soul will be made rich).

## Story arc

| Act | Scenes | Beats |
|-----|--------|-------|
| Garden hello | 1-15 | Fern's plot, Caly Sprout welcome, plan a surprise |
| Pick and wrap | 16-35 | Choose flowers, ribbon, practice quiet giggle |
| Surprise walk | 36-55 | Tiptoe path, Elder Oak nod, knock on burrow |
| Joy reveal | 56-75 | Pip opens door, gasp, hug, thank you |
| Share the bloom | 76-100 | Plant extra seeds together, celebrate garden |

## Key scenes

| # | Visual | Dialogue |
|---|--------|----------|
| 1 | Fern's colorful garden gate opens | Welcome to my garden! I'm Fern the fox. |
| 2 | Caly Sprout holds a tiny wrapped bouquet | Let's surprise Pip with flowers today. |
| 3 | Elder Oak shades the garden path | Kind surprises are like sunshine for the heart. |
| 4 | Fern tiptoes with flowers behind back | Shhh. Quiet steps make the surprise special. |
| 5 | Pip burrow door opens, eyes wide | For me? Wow! Thank you, Fern! |
| 6 | All friends plant one seed together | Sharing joy makes friendship grow. |
| 7 | Garden party wave goodbye | See you in the garden soon! |
