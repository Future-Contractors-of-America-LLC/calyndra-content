# Pip's Gentle Hello (baby, ~5 min, 50 scenes)

**Series:** Caly and Friends  
**Band:** Baby (Caly Seed, 0–23 mo)  
**Friend:** Pip the bunny  
**Mentor:** Elder Oak  
**Christian lesson:** You are wonderfully made and deeply loved (Psalm 139:14 — gentle, age-appropriate).

## Story arc

Caly Seed and Pip peek-a-boo through a soft meadow. Elder Oak shades them with gentle branches. Caregiver co-play cues invite mirroring sounds and gestures.

| Act | Scenes | Beats |
|-----|--------|-------|
| Welcome | 1–8 | Soft hello, name Pip, Elder Oak wave |
| Peek-a-boo | 9–20 | Hide, reveal, giggle, repeat with variation |
| Sounds | 21–32 | Ba, ma, gentle clap; Caly models, Pip echoes |
| Rest | 33–40 | Slow breath with Elder Oak; snuggle cue |
| Blessing | 41–50 | "You are loved" lullaby refrain; bye-bye wave |
