# Moss the Bridge Builders (child, ~15 min, 150 scenes)

**Series:** Caly and Friends  
**Band:** Child (Caly Bud, 5-8)  
**Friend:** Moss the bear  
**Mentor:** Elder Oak  
**Christian lesson:** Building bridges connects hearts across differences. (Ephesians 2:14 - Christ is our peace who breaks down walls).

## Story arc

| Act | Scenes | Beats |
|-----|--------|-------|
| Stream divide | 1-25 | Creek splits meadow, friends on two sides |
| Plan the bridge | 26-55 | Moss blueprint, gather wood, Caly Bud tools |
| Build together | 56-85 | Hammer rhythm, Reed measures, team roles |
| Cross and cheer | 86-115 | First crossing, hand-in-paw, Elder Oak blesses |
| Bridge party | 116-150 | Decorate rails, picnic both sides, song |

## Key scenes

| # | Visual | Dialogue |
|---|--------|----------|
| 1 | Wide creek, friends waving from opposite banks | We're stuck on different sides. We need a bridge. |
| 2 | Moss unfurls a hand-drawn bridge plan | Moss the bear builds bridges, not walls. |
| 3 | Caly Bud holds nails, Reed holds measuring stick | Every friend has a job. Together we can do this. |
| 4 | Elder Oak leans a sturdy branch as anchor | Strong roots hold up what friends build together. |
| 5 | Final plank placed, bridge complete | Step by step, we connected two worlds. |
| 6 | All friends cross hand in hand | Crossing together is better than staying apart. |
| 7 | Bridge decorated with flowers and a banner | Bridge builders forever! See you on the path. |
