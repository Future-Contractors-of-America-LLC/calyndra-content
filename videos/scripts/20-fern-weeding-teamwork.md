# Fern's Weeding Teamwork (toddler, ~10 min, 100 scenes)

**Series:** Caly and Friends  
**Band:** Toddler (Caly Sprout, 2-4)  
**Friend:** Fern the fox  
**Mentor:** Elder Oak  
**Christian lesson:** Working together makes hard jobs feel lighter. (Ecclesiastes 4:9 - two are better than one).

## Story arc

| Act | Scenes | Beats |
|-----|--------|-------|
| Weedy problem | 1-15 | Garden overrun, Fern sighs, call for help |
| Team forms | 16-35 | Pip, Moss invite, assign rows, tools song |
| Weed and sing | 36-55 | Pull rhythm, pile weeds, Elder Oak cheer |
| Snack break | 56-75 | Share water and berries, rest in shade |
| Clean garden | 76-100 | Reveal neat rows, plant new flowers, celebrate |

## Key scenes

| # | Visual | Dialogue |
|---|--------|----------|
| 1 | Overgrown garden patch, Fern hands on hips | Oh no! Too many weeds. I need help. |
| 2 | Caly Sprout gathers friends with a sign | Many hands make light work. Let's team up! |
| 3 | Elder Oak roots frame the garden rows | Elder Oak says friends help friends grow. |
| 4 | Fern, Pip, Moss pull weeds in a rhythm | Pull, pull, sing! Teamwork makes it fun. |
| 5 | Shade break with shared berries | Rest and share. Then we finish strong. |
| 6 | Garden neat and bright with new flowers | We did it together! What a beautiful garden. |
| 7 | Team high-five and garden gate close | Teamwork today, friendship forever. |
