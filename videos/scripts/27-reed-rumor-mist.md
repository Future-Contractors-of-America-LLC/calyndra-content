# Reed and the Rumor Mist (tween, ~20 min, 200 scenes)

**Series:** Caly and Friends  
**Band:** Tween (Caly Sprig, 9-12)  
**Friend:** Reed the owl  
**Mentor:** Elder Oak  
**Christian lesson:** Check facts before words spread like fog. (Proverbs 18:17 - first to plead his case seems right).

## Story arc

| Act | Scenes | Beats |
|-----|--------|-------|
| Misty morning | 1-30 | Fog over meadow, whispers start about Moss |
| Whisper chain | 31-70 | Rumor grows, friends avoid Moss, Reed notices |
| Perch and pause | 71-110 | Reed climbs perch, Caly Sprig fact checklist |
| Truth clears | 111-150 | Reed asks Moss directly, rumor dissolves |
| Mist lesson | 151-200 | Group talk about rumors, Elder Oak proverb |

## Key scenes

| # | Visual | Dialogue |
|---|--------|----------|
| 1 | Thick fog, friends whispering behind paws | Something feels wrong. Stories are spreading. |

| 3 | Caly Sprig holds a fact-check feather | Is it true? Is it kind? Is it needed? |
