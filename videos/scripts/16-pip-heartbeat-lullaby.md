# Pip's Heartbeat Lullaby (baby, ~5 min, 50 scenes)

**Series:** Caly and Friends  
**Band:** Baby (Caly Seed, 0-23 mo)  
**Friend:** Pip the bunny  
**Mentor:** Elder Oak  
**Christian lesson:** You are loved with a steady, never-ending heart. (Jeremiah 31:3 - loved with everlasting love).

## Story arc

| Act | Scenes | Beats |
|-----|--------|-------|
| Quiet hello | 1-8 | Soft pulse light, Caly Seed whisper hello |
| Heartbeat play | 9-20 | Tap chest rhythm, Pip echoes ba-bum sound |
| Caregiver beat | 21-32 | Grown-up heart tap mirror, slow sway |
| Lullaby rise | 33-40 | Elder Oak hum, heartbeat slows to rest |
| Sleep pulse | 41-50 | Fading heartbeat lullaby, goodnight |

## Key scenes

| # | Visual | Dialogue |

| 7 | Final soft pulse and wave | Goodnight, little heart. |
