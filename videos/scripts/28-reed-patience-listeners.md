# Reed's Patience Listeners (tween, ~20 min, 200 scenes)

**Series:** Caly and Friends  
**Band:** Tween (Caly Sprig, 9-12)  
**Friend:** Reed the owl  
**Mentor:** Elder Oak  
**Christian lesson:** Listening fully is a gift that helps others feel seen. (James 1:19 - be quick to listen, slow to speak).

## Story arc

| Act | Scenes | Beats |
|-----|--------|-------|
| Busy perch | 1-30 | Reed interrupted often, Fern needs to talk |
| Listener pledge | 31-70 | Caly Sprig challenge, no interrupt timer |
| Fern's story | 71-110 | Fern shares worry, Reed listens whole way |
| More friends | 111-150 | Pip, Moss share, Reed repeats back |
| Listener circle | 151-200 | Practice pairs, Elder Oak blessing, badges |

## Key scenes

| # | Visual | Dialogue |
|---|--------|----------|
| 1 | Reed's wisdom perch, friends lined up to talk | Everyone wants to talk. Who will listen? |
| 2 | Reed closes beak, opens ears wide | Today I practice patience listening. |
| 3 | Caly Sprig sets a sand timer on the branch | Let them finish. Then you respond. |
| 4 | Fern talks about garden worry, Reed nods | I hear you. You're afraid the frost will come. |
| 5 | Elder Oak: roots listen to rain before they drink | Deep listening helps friends grow strong. |
| 6 | Reed repeats Moss's words back accurately | Did I understand you right? Tell me more. |
| 7 | Circle of friends with listener leaf badges | Patience listeners change the whole meadow. |
