# Moss and the Kindness Trail (child, ~15 min, 150 scenes)

**Series:** Caly and Friends  
**Band:** Child (Caly Bud, 5–8)  
**Friend:** Moss the bear  
**Mentor:** Elder Oak  
**Christian lesson:** Small acts of kindness light the path (Matthew 5:16 — let your light shine).

## Story arc

| Act | Scenes | Beats |
|-----|--------|-------|
| Trailhead | 1–20 | Map, Moss backpack, quest rules |
| Obstacle 1 | 21–50 | Help a bird — HELP word power |
| Obstacle 2 | 51–80 | Share trail snack — PLEASE and THANK YOU |
| Obstacle 3 | 81–110 | Include a shy friend — COME PLAY |
| Summit | 111–150 | Kindness stones, Elder Oak story, song |
