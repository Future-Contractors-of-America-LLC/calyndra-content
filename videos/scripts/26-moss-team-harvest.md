# Moss's Team Harvest (child, ~15 min, 150 scenes)

**Series:** Caly and Friends  
**Band:** Child (Caly Bud, 5-8)  
**Friend:** Moss the bear  
**Mentor:** Elder Oak  
**Christian lesson:** Sharing the harvest blesses everyone at the table. (Acts 2:46 - broke bread with glad and generous hearts).

## Story arc

| Act | Scenes | Beats |
|-----|--------|-------|
| Harvest eve | 1-25 | Garden ripe, Moss counts baskets needed |
| Morning pick | 26-55 | Dawn harvest, roles assigned, work song |
| Sort and share | 56-85 | Divide into shares, one for neighbors |
| Community table | 86-115 | Long table setup, invite shy hedgehog |
| Harvest feast | 116-150 | Eat together, thank Elder Oak, dance |

## Key scenes

| # | Visual | Dialogue |
|---|--------|----------|
| 1 | Sunrise over full garden rows, Moss with basket | Harvest day! The garden gave us so much. |
| 2 | Friends spread through rows with baskets | Pick together, share together, feast together. |
| 3 | Moss divides vegetables into equal piles | We keep some and share some. That's how we grow. |
| 4 | Elder Oak shades a long wooden table | God gives seedtime and harvest. Give thanks. |
| 5 | Shy hedgehog invited to sit at table | There's always room for one more friend. |
| 6 | All friends hold hands before eating | Thank you for food, friends, and this day. |
| 7 | Sunset cleanup, baskets empty, hearts full | Team harvest complete. Until next season! |
