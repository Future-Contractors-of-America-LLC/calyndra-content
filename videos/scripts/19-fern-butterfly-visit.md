# Fern's Butterfly Visit (toddler, ~10 min, 100 scenes)

**Series:** Caly and Friends  
**Band:** Toddler (Caly Sprout, 2-4)  
**Friend:** Fern the fox  
**Mentor:** Elder Oak  
**Christian lesson:** Be gentle with delicate things and people. (Colossians 3:12 - clothe yourselves with gentleness).

## Story arc

| Act | Scenes | Beats |
|-----|--------|-------|
| Garden watch | 1-15 | Butterfly lands, freeze and whisper rules |
| Gentle approach | 16-35 | Slow steps, soft hands, Caly models |
| Butterfly rest | 36-55 | Sits on Fern's nose, giggle quiet, photo |
| Friend shares | 56-75 | Pip wants turn, take turns chart, Elder Oak |
| Fly away wave | 76-100 | Butterfly leaves, gratitude circle |
