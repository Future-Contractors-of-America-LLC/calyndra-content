# Reed's Honest Feather (tween, ~20 min, 200 scenes)

**Series:** Caly and Friends  
**Band:** Tween (Caly Sprig, 9-12)  
**Friend:** Reed the owl  
**Mentor:** Elder Oak  
**Christian lesson:** Honesty weighs lightly; lies grow heavy over time. (Proverbs 12:22 - lying lips are an abomination to the Lord).

## Story arc

| Act | Scenes | Beats |
|-----|--------|-------|
