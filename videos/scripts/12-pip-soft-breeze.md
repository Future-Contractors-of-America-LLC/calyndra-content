# Pip and the Soft Breeze (baby, ~5 min, 50 scenes)

**Series:** Caly and Friends  
**Band:** Baby (Caly Seed, 0-23 mo)  
**Friend:** Pip the bunny  
**Mentor:** Elder Oak  
**Christian lesson:** God's gentle touch is everywhere you feel safe. (Psalm 23:2 - green pastures and quiet waters).

## Story arc

| Act | Scenes | Beats |
|-----|--------|-------|
| Welcome | 1-8 | Caly Seed hello, meadow breeze, Pip ears twitch |
| Feel the breeze | 9-20 | Leaf dance, soft whoosh sounds, caregiver mirror |
| Peek and sway | 21-32 | Pip hides in grass, breeze reveals, giggle loop |
| Rest | 33-40 | Elder Oak shade, slow breath with breeze |
| Blessing | 41-50 | Gentle lullaby, bye-bye wave in the wind |

| # | Visual | Dialogue |
