# Moss Lost and Found (child, ~15 min, 150 scenes)

**Series:** Caly and Friends  
**Band:** Child (Caly Bud, 5-8)  
**Friend:** Moss the bear  
**Mentor:** Elder Oak  
**Christian lesson:** Helping someone find what matters shows real friendship. (Luke 15:4 - leaves the ninety-nine to find the one).

## Story arc

| Act | Scenes | Beats |
|-----|--------|-------|
| Lost ribbon | 1-25 | Pip's favorite ribbon missing, Pip sad |
| Search party | 26-55 | Moss organizes grid search, Caly Bud map |
| Clue trail | 56-85 | Feather, paw print, Reed spots from tree |
| Found in bush | 86-115 | Ribbon tangled, gentle untangle, Pip relief |
| Search celebration | 116-150 | Thank you circle, Moss medal, Elder Oak word |

## Key scenes

| # | Visual | Dialogue |
|---|--------|----------|
| 1 | Pip sniffling, empty paw where ribbon was | My ribbon is lost. It was my favorite. |
| 2 | Moss puts on explorer hat, opens notebook | Don't worry, Pip. We'll find it together. |
| 3 | Search grid drawn in dirt, friends assigned zones | Everyone has a search spot. No friend left behind. |
| 4 | Reed calls from a branch, points to bush | I see something blue near the berry bush! |
| 5 | Moss gently untangles ribbon from thorns | Found it! You never gave up on your friend. |
| 6 | Pip hugs Moss, ribbon back on ear | Thank you for looking until you found it. |
| 7 | Elder Oak gifts Moss a kindness leaf badge | Seekers of the lost are heroes in God's eyes. |
