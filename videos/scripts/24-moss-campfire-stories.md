# Moss's Campfire Stories (child, ~15 min, 150 scenes)

**Series:** Caly and Friends  
**Band:** Child (Caly Bud, 5-8)  
**Friend:** Moss the bear  
**Mentor:** Elder Oak  
**Christian lesson:** Stories connect us to wisdom passed down with love. (Deuteronomy 6:7 - teach diligently to your children).

## Story arc

| Act | Scenes | Beats |
|-----|--------|-------|
| Camp setup | 1-25 | Tent, fire ring, Moss gathers firewood |
| First story | 26-55 | Elder Oak tale of brave seed, friends listen |
| Everyone shares | 56-85 | Each friend tells one minute story, Caly Bud |
| Star gazing | 86-115 | Quiet moment, constellation game, hush |
| Story promise | 116-150 | Promise to tell stories again, marshmallow toast |

## Key scenes

| # | Visual | Dialogue |
|---|--------|----------|
| 1 | Forest clearing, campfire crackling, stars above | Welcome to Moss's campfire circle. |
| 2 | Moss in camp chair, friends on logs | Tonight we share stories that warm the heart. |
| 3 | Elder Oak's face lit by firelight | Long ago, a tiny seed trusted the soil and grew. |
| 4 | Fern tells a garden story, Pip claps | Every friend has a story worth hearing. |
| 5 | Caly Bud points at star shapes | Stories are like stars. They light up the dark. |

| 7 | Friends toast marshmallows, fire dims | Same time next week? Stories keep us close. |
