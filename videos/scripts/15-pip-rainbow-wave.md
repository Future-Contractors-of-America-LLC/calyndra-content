# Pip's Rainbow Wave (baby, ~5 min, 50 scenes)

**Series:** Caly and Friends  
**Band:** Baby (Caly Seed, 0-23 mo)  
**Friend:** Pip the bunny  
**Mentor:** Elder Oak  
**Christian lesson:** After rain comes color and hope. (Genesis 9:13 - rainbow as a sign of promise).

## Story arc

| Act | Scenes | Beats |
|-----|--------|-------|
| After rain | 1-8 | Drops stop, Caly Seed hello, Pip shakes fur |
| Color peek | 9-20 | Rainbow arc appears, wave hello to colors |
| Color dance | 21-32 | Red orange yellow play, caregiver color point |
| Rainbow rest | 33-40 | Elder Oak under arc, soft color glow |
| Promise wave | 41-50 | Rainbow bye-bye, promise of good things |

## Key scenes

| # | Visual | Dialogue |
|---|--------|----------|
| 1 | Wet meadow clearing, rainbow forming | Hello, friend! Look what came after the rain. |
| 2 | Pip hops toward rainbow colors | I'm Pip! Red, orange, yellow - hello! |
| 3 | Elder Oak framed by rainbow arc | Elder Oak says rainbows remind us of God's promise. |
| 4 | Caregiver points to each color | Your grown-up can name the colors with you. |
| 5 | Pip waves under green and blue | Wave hello to every beautiful color! |
| 6 | Rainbow fades to soft gold light | God keeps promises. You are loved. |
| 7 | Pip and Caly wave under fading arc | Bye-bye, rainbow. See you again soon. |
