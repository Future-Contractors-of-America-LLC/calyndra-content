# Laurel's Gratitude Journal (adult, ~45 min, 450 scenes)

**Series:** Caly and Friends  
**Band:** Adult (Caly Bloom, 18+)  
**Friend:** Laurel the cardinal  
**Mentor:** Elder Oak  
**Christian lesson:** Naming blessings daily reshapes how you see hard seasons. (Psalm 103:2 - forget not all His benefits).

## Story arc

| Act | Scenes | Beats |
|-----|--------|-------|
| Hard week | 1-60 | Laurel faces setbacks, mood low, Caly Bloom |
| Journal gift | 61-120 | Elder Oak gives blank journal, three-line rule |
| Day one to three | 121-210 | Small mercies listed, resistance fades |
| Hard day entry | 211-300 | Gratitude alongside grief, honest page |
| Month review | 301-390 | Read back entries, pattern of grace visible |
| Share practice | 391-450 | Laurel teaches journal at community table |

## Key scenes

| # | Visual | Dialogue |
|---|--------|----------|
| 1 | Laurel at kitchen table, rain on window, sighing | This week felt like one long gray cloud. |
| 2 | Elder Oak places a simple bound journal on table | Three lines each night. Blessings you almost missed. |
| 3 | Laurel writes: warm tea, Pip's giggle, hot shower | Small things. But my chest feels lighter. |
| 4 | Hard news day, Laurel writes through tears | Gratitude doesn't erase pain. It sits beside it. |
| 5 | Laurel reads a month of entries aloud to Caly | Look how many mercies hid in ordinary days. |
| 6 | Community table, Laurel passes journals to friends | Try three lines tonight. See what changes. |
| 7 | Laurel closes journal with a smile at the stars | I won't forget. Thank You for today. |
