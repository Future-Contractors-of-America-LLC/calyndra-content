# Pip's Twinkle Toes (baby, ~5 min, 50 scenes)

**Series:** Caly and Friends  
**Band:** Baby (Caly Seed, 0-23 mo)  
**Friend:** Pip the bunny  
**Mentor:** Elder Oak  
**Christian lesson:** Your body is a gift to move with joy. (Psalm 139:14 - fearfully and wonderfully made).

## Story arc

| Act | Scenes | Beats |
|-----|--------|-------|
| Hello feet | 1-8 | Caly Seed points to toes, Pip wiggles |
| Wiggle dance | 9-20 | Toe twinkle song, caregiver toe-tap mirror |
| Hop and pause | 21-32 | Small hops, giggle, repeat with variation |
| Tired toes | 33-40 | Slow toe wiggle, Elder Oak rest shade |
| Toe blessing | 41-50 | Wonderfully made refrain, bye-bye wiggle |

## Key scenes

| # | Visual | Dialogue |
|---|--------|----------|
| 1 | Sunny patch, Caly Seed sits, Pip hops in | Hello! Let's wiggle our twinkle toes. |
| 2 | Close-up Pip feet wiggling | I'm Pip! Watch my toes go twinkle-twinkle. |
| 3 | Elder Oak root bench, warm light | Elder Oak says every part of you is special. |
| 4 | Caregiver taps toes gently | Your grown-up can wiggle toes with you. |
| 5 | Pip hops in a tiny circle | Hop, hop! God made you to move with joy. |
| 6 | Slow wiggle, toes rest | Twinkle... rest. You did so well. |
| 7 | Pip waves a foot goodbye | Bye-bye, twinkle toes! |
