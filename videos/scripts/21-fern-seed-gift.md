# Fern's Seed Gift (toddler, ~10 min, 100 scenes)

**Series:** Caly and Friends  
**Band:** Toddler (Caly Sprout, 2-4)  
**Friend:** Fern the fox  
**Mentor:** Elder Oak  
**Christian lesson:** Giving something you love can grow more love. (2 Corinthians 9:7 - God loves a cheerful giver).

## Story arc

| Act | Scenes | Beats |
|-----|--------|-------|
| Special seeds | 1-15 | Fern's favorite seed packet, hard to share |
| Gift idea | 16-35 | Caly suggests giving, Fern thinks, Elder Oak |
| Wrap and walk | 36-55 | Decorate packet, walk to Moss's house |
| Give and plant | 56-75 | Moss thanks Fern, plant side by side |
| Double bloom | 76-100 | Both gardens have same flower, celebrate |

## Key scenes

| # | Visual | Dialogue |
|---|--------|----------|
| 1 | Fern holds a shiny seed packet close | These are my favorite seeds. I love them. |
| 2 | Caly Sprout kneels beside Fern | What if sharing them grows something even better? |
| 3 | Elder Oak drops an acorn near Fern's paw | A gift given freely comes back in friendship. |
| 4 | Fern decorates the packet with a ribbon | For you, Moss. I want you to grow these too. |
| 5 | Moss hugs Fern at his garden gate | Thank you! I'll take good care of them. |
| 6 | Two matching flowers bloom in two gardens | Now we both have beauty because you shared. |
| 7 | Fern waves from her gate at sunset | Sharing seeds planted joy in two places. |
