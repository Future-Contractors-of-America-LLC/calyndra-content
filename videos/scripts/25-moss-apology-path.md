# Moss and the Apology Path (child, ~15 min, 150 scenes)

**Series:** Caly and Friends  
**Band:** Child (Caly Bud, 5-8)  
**Friend:** Moss the bear  
**Mentor:** Elder Oak  
**Christian lesson:** Saying sorry and forgiving mends what was broken. (Colossians 3:13 - forgive as the Lord forgave you).

## Story arc

| Act | Scenes | Beats |
|-----|--------|-------|
| The break | 1-25 | Moss accidentally breaks Fern's pot, Fern upset |
| Cool down | 26-55 | Separate paths, Caly Bud breathing, Elder Oak |
| Apology walk | 56-85 | Moss walks apology path, practices words |
| I'm sorry | 86-115 | Moss apologizes, Fern listens, repair together |
| New pot | 116-150 | Build new pot, plant together, path complete |

## Key scenes

| # | Visual | Dialogue |
|---|--------|----------|
| 1 | Broken clay pot, Fern with tears, Moss looking down | I didn't mean to break it. Fern is so sad. |
| 2 | Caly Bud guides Moss to a quiet bench | Before we fix the pot, let's calm our hearts. |
| 3 | Elder Oak points to a winding stone path | The apology path helps us say what matters. |
| 4 | Moss walks path rehearsing: I am sorry | Sorry means I care that I hurt you. |
| 5 | Moss kneels before Fern with new clay | I'm sorry I broke your pot. Can we fix it together? |
| 6 | Fern nods, both hands shape new pot | I forgive you. Let's make something new. |
| 7 | New pot planted, friends walk path again smiling | Repair makes friendship stronger than before. |
